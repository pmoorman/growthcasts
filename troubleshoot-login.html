Let's sort out what is going wrong with your login

Hi there, Caspar here.
Let me try to explain what goes wrong and how to fix it

Our platform runs on cookies, if you have the correct cookie (which you get once you buy our program), it'll let you login, otherwise the page just refreshes.

Now sometimes, something goes wrong and you get the wrong cookies. Now even logging in with the correct email address is not going to let you in (extra security measures).

---

How to fix it?

To fix it you have to delete your cookies or try using another browser (Chrome/Safari/Edge/Firefox).

To delete your cookies, follow the steps below

You can delete all your cookies (clear browser history -> all cookies from last 60 days), or just the relevant ones:

  1. First close all windows that have our websites open.
  2. Then go to the settings of your browser and look for 'cookies' [in Chrome Settings: Site settings>>Cookies and data>>See all cookies and data]
  3. Here you can find and delete your cookies, like this:
  4. Search and delete the following cookies:
    1. Type in 'Elite', and click 'remove all shown'
    2. Type in 'Double', and delete all the double-agency.com cookies
    3. Type in 'Drip' (our backend email system) and click on 'Remove all shown'
    4. Type in 'Netlify' (our backend website system) and click 'Remove all shown'
